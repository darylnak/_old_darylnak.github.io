I use lists when grocery shopping. I need:

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Sausage
  • Underwear (6pack - white)

Now I can make a killer omelet; here's how to do it:

  1. Heat a small pan ( medium heat )
  2. Lightly coat bottom of pan with butter
  3. Crack eggs in a bowl, whisk, and pour into pan
  4. Add chopped sausage to eggs
  5. Flip
  6. Serve!
